Mandarin Word Test Instructions

Hi! This is a test for Mandarin words. There are sixty words in this test. Each word consists of two characters, and you will both see and hear the Mandarin words. Some of the items are real Mandarin words, for example, 科學 (kē xué) ‘science’, and some of them are not real Mandarin words.

When you see and hear a word, you need to decide whether it is a word or a nonword in Mandarin Chinese.

  • If you think it is a word in Mandarin Chinese (either you know the word or you have heard of it but cannot recall its meaning), please click the button “a Mandarin word”.
  • If you think it is not a word in Mandarin Chinese, please click the button “NOT a Mandarin word”.

This test is not timed. Please answer based on your intuition and respond as quickly as possible without overthinking.

Please complete this test on your own without consulting any Mandarin Chinese dictionary.

All words are presented in Traditional Chinese characters, and the audio recordings are in a Taiwanese Mandarin accent.
